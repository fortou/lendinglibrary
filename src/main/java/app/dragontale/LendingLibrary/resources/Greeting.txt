Welcome to the Lending Library.

Use the following links to navigate and test the app's
features. Replace asterisks with your input.

Retrieve library book list: (Unavailable)
http://localhost:8888/books/list

Retrieve information about a book:
http://localhost:8888/books/?=bookID=*

Retrieve information about a person:
http://localhost:8888/people/?=personID=*&pinCode=*

Retrieve information about a lending: (Available only to staff)
http://localhost:8888/lendings/?=lendingID=*&personID=*&pinCode=*
